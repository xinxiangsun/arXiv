### [1] [On the Origin of Mass Ejection in Failed Supernovae](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05289)
*Daniel A. Paradiso,Sarah Vallejo,Eric R. Coughlin*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 本文研究了失败超新星（FSNe）中弱激波的稳定性与演化，发现高马赫数解不稳定，激波马赫数随时间增长偏离自相似解；低马赫数解稳定。当质量损失超过临界值时，激波会趋近强激波极限。结果表明，中微子引起的相对质量损失和激波形成处的恒星密度梯度决定了激波强度与抛射物质的能力，这解释了为何红超巨星比致密前身星更易产生明亮爆发。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解失败超新星中弱激波的传播特性及其稳定性，揭示决定激波强度和物质抛射能力的关键物理因素，进而解释不同前身星（如红超巨星与致密星）爆发亮度的差异。

Method: 通过自相似解分析，研究不同马赫数激波的稳定性；结合数值方法，分析质量损失对激波演化的影响，并比较不同前身星结构下的激波行为。

Result: 高马赫数解不稳定，马赫数随时间以∝t^α (α≲0.1)增长偏离自相似解；低马赫数解稳定。存在临界质量损失，超过该值激波趋近强激波极限。相对质量损失（中微子损失质量与激波包围质量之比）及激波形成处的密度梯度决定激波强度。

Conclusion: 红超巨星因其在激波形成时的相对质量损失远超临界值，更易抛射物质并产生更明亮的爆发；而致密前身星则相反。这为理解失败超新星的可观测信号提供了理论依据。

Abstract: Some high-mass stars likely end their lives in underluminous implosions that leave behind a black hole, known as failed supernovae (FSNe). However, neutrinos radiated during proto-neutron star formation generate a weak (Mach $\gtrsim 1$) shockwave in the outer layers of the star, which produces a unique transient as it breaks out of the dying star and signals its imminent disappearance. It was recently shown that there are two self-similar solutions that describe the propagation of this weak shockwave, and these solutions simultaneously contain outward-moving ejecta and fallback accretion onto the black hole. Here we show that the larger Mach number solutions are unstable, such that the Mach number of the shock grows with time $t$ and deviates from the self-similar prediction as $\propto t^α$, with $α\lesssim 0.1$, whereas the smaller Mach number solutions are stable. We also show that, above a critical mass loss that is readily achievable in core-collapse supernovae, the shock asymptotically strengthens and approaches the strong limit. Our results imply that it is the mass lost to neutrinos \textit{relative} to the mass enclosed by the shockwave, as well as the stellar density gradient where the shock forms, that primarily dictate its strength and the amount of material it ejects. These criteria explain why red supergiants, which have relative mass losses well in excess of the critical value at the time of shock formation, more readily eject material and create more luminous explosions compared to more compact progenitors.

### [2] [No model-independent evidence for a peak in binary black hole spin (mis)alignments](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05300)
*Noah E. Wolfe,Salvatore Vitale,Michael Zevin*

Main category: astro-ph.HE

TL;DR: 研究分析了LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA第四次引力波瞬变目录，发现自旋倾斜分布的峰值不具备统计显著性且不独立于模型；进一步分析表明自旋倾斜与黑洞质量之间无显著相关性，但确认了自旋大小与质量之间存在可靠相关性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 黑洞自旋轨道错位程度（倾斜角）是区分孤立双星、动力学环境或层级三合星等不同双星形成渠道的关键诊断工具，但受限于模型误设、数值方差和泊松方差，以及单事件倾斜角测量精度差，导致群体层面的自旋倾斜测量困难。此前有研究报道自旋倾斜分布可能存在峰值，激发了对该问题的进一步检验。

Method: 利用第四次LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA引力波瞬变目录，对不同黑洞质量下的自旋取向偏好进行统计检验，评估自旋倾斜分布峰值的统计显著性和模型依赖性；同时采用互补方法，在固定质量尺度上测量自旋大小与倾斜分布，检验质量与自旋属性之间的相关性。

Result: 自旋倾斜分布中的峰值不具备统计显著性，且结果依赖模型假设；自旋倾斜与黑洞质量之间未发现可靠相关性；但确认了自旋大小与质量之间存在显著相关性，与近期分析结果一致。

Conclusion: 当前引力波数据尚不能可靠基于自旋倾斜属性识别子群体，自旋倾斜并非区分双星形成渠道的稳健诊断工具；自旋大小与质量的相关性得到确认，可为双星演化研究提供更可靠的观测约束。

Abstract: The degree of black-hole spin-orbit misalignment ("tilts") in the astrophysical population could be a powerful diagnostic to distinguish between binary formation in isolation, in dynamical environments, or in hierarchical triples. However, robust population-level spin tilt measurements are complicated by model misspecification as well as numerical and Poisson variance, ultimately owing to poor single-event constraints on tilts. Motivated by reports of a possible peak in the spin tilt distribution, we analyze the fourth LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA gravitational-wave transient catalog to test for preferred spin orientations at different black hole masses. We find that a peak in spin tilts is not statistically significant nor model independent. Since the data cannot be used to reliably identify subpopulations based on their spin tilt properties, we also consider a complementary approach: measuring the spin magnitude and tilt distributions at fixed mass scales. We find no confident correlation between mass and spin tilt, but we do confirm a confident correlation between spin magnitude and mass, corroborating recent analyses.

  <summary>Details</summary>

Conclusion: X射线偏振为高能天体物理提供了重要的新观测维度，相关探测技术正在快速发展，将推动对天体物理系统结构和磁场的新认识。

Abstract: X-ray polarimetry is now providing a new way to look at the high energy sky. The addition of two observables, polarization fraction and angle, reveals crucial new information on the structure of accretion flows and magnetic fields in astrophysical systems. Here, we review the basic physical processes that produce polarized X-rays in astrophysical contexts. Then, we briefly describe the physical processes used to measure X-ray polarization and the detectors that have been flown or are under construction.
